IDEAFARM.COM Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy as we would expect our own privacy to be respected by any decent person:

  1. We treat all persons with respect and as we ourselves want to be treated.
  2. Our business is to serve you (by creating value for you), not to exploit you while serving some third party.
  3. For example, we are not in the business of generating revenue by displaying third party advertisements to you.
  4. We also do not sell or otherwise disclose your personal information.
  5. We find new customers in ways that are not intrusive.
  6. For example, we do not purchase email or postal addresses or telephone numbers.
  7. If we send you general or sales information, you will always be able to easily and immediately "opt out".
