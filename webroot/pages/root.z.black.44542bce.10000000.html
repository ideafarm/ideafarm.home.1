Users of the IdeaNet (tm) Book Distribution System comprise a "trust community".

By paying a significant set up fee, accepting the "NO REFUNDS" policy, and providing a verifiable shipping address, users make themselves accountable within the system. In return, users enjoy exclusive "trusted status" as they access the college and the nonprofit membership corporation, which cannot be otherwise accessed. The purpose of the system is not just to distribute "books"; it is designed to gather persons into a single worldwide community based upon accountability and trust, and into wo or more special interest communities based upon common interest. Each of these common interest communities can be thought of as a book discussion group focused on wo of the books in the IdeaNet (tm) system.
